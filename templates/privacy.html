Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. You can delete your account at any time, which permanently removes all associated data from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not share your personal or financial data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information on our website. You may also review our Terms and Conditions.
